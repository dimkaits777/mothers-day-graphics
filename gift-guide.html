Gift Guide 2026

10 Quick & Easy Mother's Day Gifts You Can Create Today

From greeting cards to custom mugs — find the best projects to personalize with beautiful digital graphics.

The best gifts aren't the most expensive. They're the ones where someone took time, thought, and creativity. This Mother's Day, skip the gift cards and make something she'll keep forever.

🎨 Pro tip: All designs below come with instant download and include PNG, SVG, EPS, and more. Compatible with Cricut, Silhouette, Canva, Photoshop, and all major design tools. Browse all designs here →
1

Handmade Greeting Card

A personalized greeting card is the gift that melts every mother's heart. Print your chosen graphic on premium cardstock, add a handwritten note, and present it with love. She'll keep it on her shelf for years.

  • ✅ Best paper: 300gsm cardstock or watercolor paper
  • ✅ Best size: A5 folded card (148×210mm)
  • ✅ Add embossing, ribbon, or washi tape for extra charm
Browse Floral Designs →
2

Personalized Coffee Mug

Transfer a "Best Mom Ever" or cute animal design onto a white ceramic mug using sublimation or heat-transfer vinyl. She'll think of you with every morning coffee. It's the gift that keeps on giving — every single day.

  • ✅ Use sublimation ink and a white ceramic mug
  • ✅ Heat press at 400°F for 60 seconds
  • ✅ Or use a local print-on-demand service
Browse Sublimation Designs →
3

Framed Art Print

Choose a beautiful quote or watercolor design, print it on fine art paper (A4 or A3), and frame it. An elegant piece for her bedroom, living room, or home office. The kind of gift that says "I know your style."

  • ✅ Fine art paper or photo lustre paper
  • ✅ Frame with mat board for a gallery look
  • ✅ Quote designs work beautifully as wall art
Browse Quote Designs →
4

Canvas Tote Bag

Canvas tote bags are a daily staple — grocery runs, farmers markets, beach days. Print or iron-on a beautiful design on natural-colored canvas. She'll carry it everywhere and think of you every time.

  • ✅ Use iron-on transfer paper or HTV vinyl
  • ✅ Best designs: large florals or bold lettering
  • ✅ 12 oz natural cotton canvas works best
Browse Floral Designs →
5

Memory Scrapbook

Use clipart, borders, and quote graphics to create beautiful scrapbook layouts. Add family photos for pages she'll flip through for years. A truly personal keepsake.

  • ✅ Print designs on sticker paper or photo paper
  • ✅ Combine with washi tape and handwriting
  • ✅ Clipart bundles give you the most variety
Browse Clipart Bundles →
6

Custom T-Shirt or Hoodie

Iron-on or sublimate a fun design onto a t-shirt or cozy hoodie. "Mama Bear," "Super Mom," "Dog Mom" — there's a design for every kind of mom.

  • ✅ Use HTV vinyl with Cricut or Silhouette
  • ✅ Mirror the design before cutting!
  • ✅ Works great on cotton and poly-blend fabrics
Browse Sublimation Designs →
7

Decorative Throw Pillow

A personalized throw pillow instantly elevates any living room. Print a watercolor design on a linen pillow cover, add a pillow insert, and gift it in a beautiful box for a boutique-quality present.

  • ✅ Use sublimation on polyester pillow covers
  • ✅ Standard throw pillow: 18×18 inches
  • ✅ Floral and watercolor designs work best
Browse Watercolor Designs →
8

Cake Topper & Party Decor

Print, cut, and mount designs on sticks for cake toppers, cupcake wraps, or party banners. Perfect for a Mother's Day brunch celebration!

  • ✅ Print on heavy cardstock for durability
  • ✅ Use wooden sticks and craft glue
  • ✅ Combine multiple small designs for a set
Browse Kids Designs →
9

Gift for Grandma

Grammy, Nana, Mimi, Oma — grandma names are deeply personal. Find a design with her specific grandma name and put it on a mug, towel, or framed print. She'll love that you remembered her special name.

  • ✅ We have designs for every grandma name
  • ✅ Pair with a photo of the grandkids
  • ✅ Great on kitchen towels and aprons
Browse Grandma Designs →
10

Digital Photo Collage

Combine family photos with decorative frames and clipart in Canva or Photoshop. Print or share digitally — a modern keepsake that tells your family's story beautifully.

  • ✅ Use Canva (free) or Photoshop
  • ✅ Mix family photos with decorative elements
  • ✅ Print on canvas for a gallery-quality gift
Browse Modern Designs →
